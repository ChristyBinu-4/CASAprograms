Palakkad, or Palghat, is a city in Kerala, a state in southwestern India.
The 18th-century Palakkad Fort has sturdy battlements, a moat and
a Hanuman temple on its grounds. North, on the Kalpathy River, the
15th-century Viswanatha Swamy Temple is the main venue of the famous
Ratholsavam chariot festival. Northeast, near Malampuzha Dam, the town
of Malampuzha has a rock garden created from recycled materials.
